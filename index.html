How it works

Three steps. About 30 seconds. Nails that actually fit, forever after.

01
Snap One Finger at a Time
Place each finger flat next to a quarter, nail facing up. The camera guides you through all five — the whole thing takes about 2 minutes.
02
The Scanner Does the Math
Computer vision uses the quarter as a real-world reference to calculate your exact nail width for every finger. More accurate than any tape measure, ruler, or guesswork.
03
Sizes Sent Instantly
Your complete size profile goes straight to your nail tech — no DMs, no typing, no guessing. Just press-ons that actually fit, every single time.
